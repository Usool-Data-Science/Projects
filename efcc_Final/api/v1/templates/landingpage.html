    • Who Are We?

    The Economic and Financial Crimes Commission (EFCC) Nigeria is a premier law enforcement agency dedicated to combating economic and financial crimes within Nigeria. Established in 2003, the EFCC has a broad mandate to investigate and prosecute financial crimes such as fraud, money laundering, embezzlement, and corruption. The commission operates with the core mission of ensuring accountability and transparency in both public and private sectors, thereby fostering economic stability and growth in Nigeria. We are committed to safeguarding the Nigerian economy from illicit financial activities through robust investigation and enforcement strategies. We work in close collaboration with other national and international agencies to track and apprehend individuals and entities involved in financial crimes. Through its efforts, we aim to promote integrity, uphold the rule of law, and enhance the overall economic wellbeing of the nation.

    Our Mission

    We are committed to investigating and prosecuting individuals and organizations involved in activities such as fraud, money laundering, embezzlement, and corruption. Our goal is to eradicate these crimes to ensure a fair and just economic environment for all Nigerians. We strive to promote accountability and transparency in both the public and private sectors. By enforcing stringent anti-corruption measures and ensuring adherence to financial regulations, we aim to build a culture of integrity and ethical conduct. Our efforts are directed towards fostering an environment where honesty and transparency are the norms, contributing to the overall governance and development of Nigeria. We help create a secure and conducive atmosphere for business operations and investments. Our work supports the nation's economic policies and initiatives, thereby contributing to sustainable development and prosperity. We empower individuals and communities to recognize, report, and resist economic and financial crimes, fostering a more vigilant and informed society.

    Our Vision

    The vision of the Economic and Financial Crimes Commission (EFCC) Nigeria is to be a model law enforcement agency, leading the fight against economic and financial crimes both nationally and globally. We aspire to build a society free of corruption and financial malfeasance, where integrity and accountability are the norms. Through innovative strategies, robust enforcement of laws, and a commitment to transparency, we aim to restore public confidence in Nigeria's financial systems and contribute to the nation's overall economic stability and growth. Our vision is to create a future where every Nigerian can thrive in an environment of fairness, justice, and prosperity.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    The Economic and Financial Crimes Commission (EFCC) is a Nigerian law enforcement agency that investigates and prosecutes economic and financial crimes such as fraud, money laundering, and corruption.

    You can report a financial crime to the EFCC through our official website, by visiting any of our offices, or by calling our hotline. We take all reports seriously and ensure confidentiality for whistleblowers.

    The EFCC investigates a wide range of economic and financial crimes, including fraud, money laundering, embezzlement, tax evasion, bribery, and corruption. We also investigate cybercrimes and financial terrorism.

    The EFCC has measures in place to protect the identity and safety of whistleblowers. We ensure that reports can be made anonymously and that all information provided is handled with the utmost confidentiality.

    You can verify the legitimacy of EFCC operations by contacting our official channels or visiting our website. We also publish press releases and updates on our investigations and activities regularly.

    Penalties for financial crimes in Nigeria can vary depending on the severity of the offense. They can range from fines and restitution to lengthy prison sentences. The EFCC works diligently to ensure that perpetrators are brought to justice.

    You can stay informed about EFCC activities by following our official social media channels, subscribing to our newsletter, and visiting our website regularly for updates on our operations and initiatives.
